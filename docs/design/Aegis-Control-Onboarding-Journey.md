# Aegis Control — Onboarding Journey

Aligned to the **Digital Governance Control System (DGCS)** model:

```
Upload → Extract → Assess → Act → Control Loop
```

Not assessment-only — this is **state ingestion that activates continuous governance control**.

**Related:** [Product Copy](./Aegis-Control-Product-Copy.md) · [Identity System](./Aegis-Control-Identity.md)

---

## Step 01 — Register & Upload

Create your secure Aegis Control workspace and upload governance artifacts.

**Supported formats:** PDF, DOCX, TXT, Markdown

All data is processed inside a **secure, isolated zone**, ensuring full confidentiality of client information.

> This is where your **governance reality enters the system**.

---

## Step 02 — AI Extraction (Entity Intelligence)

Aegis Control's semantic engine transforms unstructured content into structured governance entities.

It identifies:

- Requirements
- Controls
- Processes
- Implicit practices (not just documented ones)

> Not what your team *claims* — what actually exists.

---

## Step 03 — Maturity Scoring (State Assessment)

Extracted entities are evaluated against the Aegis Control maturity framework:

```
Initial → Managed → Defined → Quantitative → Optimizing
```

Each domain is scored with:

- Precision
- Cross-entity consistency
- Evidence-backed reasoning

> This defines your **current governance state**.

---

## Step 04 — Actionable Insights (Gap → Control)

You don't just get a score. You receive:

- A prioritized remediation roadmap
- Exact control gaps linked to standards
- Required actions to reach the next maturity level

Mapped to:

- Requirements
- SLA implications
- Evidence expectations

> This defines your **required state transformation**.

---

## Step 05 — Review & Collaborate

Explore results in a shared governance workspace:

- Maturity breakdown by domain
- Control-level gaps
- Evidence-based validation
- Team collaboration on remediation

> This is where governance becomes **operational**.

---

## Step 06 — Enforce & Restore (DGCS Loop Begins)

Once artifacts are generated and deployed:

```
SET → OBSERVE → COMPARE → ACT → VERIFY
```

Aegis Control activates continuous control:

- Monitoring rules are applied
- Multi-source evidence is validated
- Divergence triggers controlled remediation
- Verified state is restored and proven

> Onboarding ends — **control begins**.

---

## Maturity Model

### Level 1 — Initial

Processes are ad-hoc and reactive. Unpredictable outcomes, reliance on individual expertise, no systemic governance enforcement.

### Level 2 — Managed

Basic controls exist at the project level. Defined project practices, repeatability under stable conditions, limited cross-project consistency.

### Level 3 — Defined

Organization-wide governance standards are established. Standardized processes, proactive management, formalized procedures and tooling.

### Level 4 — Quantitatively Managed

Processes are controlled through measurable data. Defined KPIs and thresholds, statistical performance tracking, predictable outcomes.

### Level 5 — Optimizing

Continuous improvement driven by evidence and feedback loops. Real-time adaptation, data-driven optimization, systematic innovation.

> Level 5 = **continuous verification + controlled correction** (DGCS target state).
